Born in Ga-phaahla and raised in Soshanguve. Soshanguve is a township situated about 45 km north of Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa. It was established in 1974 on land scheduled to be incorporated into a bantustan bordering on Mabopane in Bophuthatswana, to Sotho, Shangaan, Nguni and Venda people (thus the name) who were resettled from Wallmasthal after being forcibly removed from their land. Schools in Soshanguve, i.e. Wallmansthal high and Khutso primary, are originally from Wallmasthal. The first section that was built in Soshanguve is block "K", housing residents of Wallmansthal.
